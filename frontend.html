When you browse our store, our Module 5 AI engine silently learns your preferences and delivers a completely personalized shopping experience — in real time.

✨

Reward-Guided Feedback Loop (RG-CFM)
Every time you accept, reject, or ignore a nudge, our system logs it as a reward signal. These signals are aggregated and used to retrain Module 4 (Flowboost) — making predictions smarter over time. Accepted nudge = reward 1.0, Ignored = 0.3, Rejected = 0.0.
